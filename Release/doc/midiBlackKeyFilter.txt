midiBlackKeyFilter

Separately adjust velocity of black and white keys, and/or send them on different channels.

Parameters:
•	InputCh – Channel to affect: Any, or 1-16
•	WhiteCh – Channel to send white keys on: No Change, or 1-16
•	WhiteVel – Velocity adjustment in percent for white keys (0-200%)
•	BlackCh - Channel to send black keys on: No Change, or 1-16
•	BlackVel - Velocity adjustment in percent for black keys (0-200%)
